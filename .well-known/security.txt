# Security Policy

If you discover any security issues with this website, please report them responsibly.
Please contact me through LinkedIn or GitHub with details of the vulnerability.

# Scope

This security policy applies to:
- https://nepiskopos.github.io (primary domain)
- All associated subdomains and resources

# Out of Scope

- Social engineering attacks
- Denial of service attacks
- Issues in third-party services (GitHub Pages infrastructure)

# Response

I aim to respond to security reports within 5 business days.
